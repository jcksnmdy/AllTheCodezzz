To play: Select the team that will be going by clicking on the team that is going to go on the scorboard. Then click "Randomize Words" and drag each word onto a card (easiest on the left and hardest on the right). When you are ready, click "Start" and try and act out each card (for a harder game, try not to use sounds). While you play, click the yellow when your team guesses correctly before the card disapears! When your done, click "Done" to reset.
